  • The Legends of Zelda:Tears of the Kingdom Review -

    ★★★★★ 5/5

    A literal masterpiece. If I ever got the chance meet the creator of this game, I would give him a million high fives. I just love how you can really let your creative side run free. By using the new abilities, you can build anything you can imagine. Also, the storyline is so compelling. I won't give any spoilers, but I must say that it really does play with your emotions. The cutscenes are pretty darn cinematic for a videogame. In addition, the map is HUGE! It is technically the same map as in Breath of the Wild, but there are so many little changes that it seems almost like a whole new world. There is so much stuff to explore! From equipment to caves to shrines to even the sky, I couldn't possibly see it all! The combat system is also good. The boss battles are never easy, always different, and always make you feel accomplished at the end. I've been going on and on about all this, but I haven't even talked about one of the best parts of the game: puzzles. The puzzles are so well made! Some may seem difficult, but, with hard work(and the internet), you can beat any puzzle! I could go on and on about so many more components of this wonderful game, but then we would be here forever! Overall, this game is 5/5.
