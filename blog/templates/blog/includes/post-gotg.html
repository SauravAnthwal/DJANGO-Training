  • Marvel Guardians of the Galaxy Review -

    ★★★★ 4/5

    Entertaining game! And hands down, visually (and maybe sounding) the best Marvel video game so far! Excellent balance between shooter, puzzle, comedy, Marvel lore, RPG, story, and strategy! The 80s classic rock Soundtrack is perfect! It is almost like playing a movie, it immerses you into the GotG world with cinematic gameplay brilliance! I wish now all video games follow suit! Like other reviews, it is meant to be a 1 player game, but what if they somehow make a sequel that is split-screen couch co-op? I had give it six stars!! In a strange, own opinion, I feel that you could play this game with the volume cranked up and a crowd watching it and helping you along with the puzzles, dialog choosing, and strategy choices, while enjoying watching this game as it plays out like a soap opera or “dromedy” flick and enjoy the game just as much as the one holding the game controller. Nevertheless, super fun and entertaining to play through once, yet a high re-playability score from me.
